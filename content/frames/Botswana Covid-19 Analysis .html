Introduction to Covid-19¶

Corona Virus now known as Covid-19 is a new form of virus that can cause the body to have severe problems such as Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS-CoV) which most peole know as the common flu and coughing etc.

We now have a new form of SAR-Cov-2 of this Corona Virus which most scientists discovered sometimes mid 2019. It is a very contagious disease as it easily spread from contacts with persons infected, or anything that an infected had contact with.

As we all know from the news the disease broke around December 2019 in Wuhan, China before spreading across the whole world, ultimately as we are now aware affecting us in Botswana too. Currently as it is WHO has declared a pandemic because of it lethal nature we witnessing across the world.
